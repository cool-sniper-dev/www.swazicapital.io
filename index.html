Executive Summary

Swazi Capital (Pty) Ltd is an independent corporate finance advisory boutique, with offices in Johannesburg. A black owned corporate advisory firm, run by a group of specialists with various skills in the areas of investment banking, corporate finance and property development. We are a multidisciplinary team of professionals including Economists, CA(SA) Accountants, Corporate Financiers, Marketers, Engineers and Strategists. Through our investment and development company, we specialize in various public and private investments within the South African infrastructure markets and in catalytic urban property / housing developments.

Our Objectives

We aim to deliver solutions that are suitable to finance all the bulk infrastructure required through our DFIs and Pension Fund partnerships. Our key focus is to provide flexible and competitive financing of infrastructure through blended finance to enhance affordability ....

  • Provide longer tenor.
  • Reduce transaction costs.

Challenges & Solutions

The recurring theme in (large-scale) catalytic urban property / housing developments is a lack of bulk infrastructure. The provision of the bulk infrastructure is the responsibility of the municipality There are various contributing factors: ....

  • Limited funding available for bulk infrastructure
  • A lack of bankable projects / programmes.
  • A lack of borrowing capacity of municipalities
  • A lack of integrated planning (“silo mentality”)
  • AReactionary development paradigm rather than a controlled/planned and pro-active paradigm.
  • How can we overcome these factors to enable catalytic developments and to stimulate/facilitate development?
  • We can unlock funding solution for development and implementation of bulk services being needed through various banks and the DFI’s and unlock catalytic property / housing developments which will lead to private sector investments and in turn stimulate and promote economic growth in Metros and Intermediate Cities
